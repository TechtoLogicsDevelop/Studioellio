Learn more about the project

Homezy, an online delivery platform, provides fresh, hygienic, and affordable home cooked food. It comes to the rescue of those who desire healthy, home meals but lack the time or skills to prepare one themselves.

With an aim to support small local businesses, the platform empowers home chefs by providing them a marketplace to monetize their skills.


We created an identity for them inspired by local handmade elements that are familiar and relatable like street art, hand painted signages and posters, to induce and create a nostalgic, organic and homely identity.

The logo speaks to the soul of the brand that is to support and uplift local businesses. The slight arch represents the brand's idea to lift the home chefs by providing them and their talent a platform.


The brand uses yellow, often associated with hunger, as its most prominent colour inspired from our local posters and graphics. However, the identity includes a larger colour palette complementing the Indian culture.


We move away from the vector style illustration which is commonly followed in online food delivery apps and created a rooted hand done chalk style that is organic and handmade with love just like the food offered on Homezy.

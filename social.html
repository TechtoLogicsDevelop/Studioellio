Social Offline - The indoor inspired by the outdoor
Services
 
Print Media     Communication Design

Social Offline, a blend of workspace and cafe, has managed to capture the vernacular India in a contemporary manner through its uniquely engaging interiors and collaterals.

Its Mumbai outlet required a poster campaign for their happy hours and upcoming 'Peddlers Club' event in association with a cycling society called Mumbai Riders. We pitched a series of designs in response to this.


Drinks Offer
The happy hours graphics were inspired by the city's rush hours at local stations and public transport to fill the dead time slot of 4pm to 8pm, get additional footfall in, upsell and increase liquor sales. It was used as a reminder that customers should rush to social for the happy hours!


The Peddlers Club
The brief was to inform the audience about ‘Social x Mumbai Riders: The Peddlers Club (Edition 1)’ and why they should participate in the event.


The graphics for this were inspired by the different kinds of cyclists and street signs we see across the city. It’s a call to all cyclists — whether a Romeo Rider or a Tour de Mumbai rider — to come be a part of the cycling club, and enjoy a ride on the empty streets of Mumbai.

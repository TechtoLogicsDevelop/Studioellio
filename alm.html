Packaging Design

"With Purity as its core value, the brand's aim is to keep ethnic mithai and desserts as one of the quintessential elements of festivities and culture. To follow it, the fresh new visual language has been created by making custom motifs inspired by sweets and savoury forms, and their transformation into unique patterns for each product range. Learn more about the project

Journeying over 30 years, Almond House has developed a unique style in shaping wholesome and decadent experiences. With a wide range of high quality Indian Mithai (sweets) and Namkeens (savouries) made from carefully picked ingredients and with a keen eye for craftsmanship.


When Anomaly Brands was approached to do their rebranding, it meant taking their experience and establishing them as the tastemakers of Hyderabad. The packaging is inspired by the diverse forms of Indian sweets and culture in an exclusive and identifiable way.


Almond House, the brand which specialises as the tastemaker of festivity and celebration, had to be set apart from its competitors with a fresh, contemporary



yet culturally-rooted identity. The design langauge was created keeping the evolving Indian ethinic audience in mind.

"With Purity as its core value, the brand's aim is to keep ethnic mithai and desserts as one of the quintessential elements of festivities and culture. To follow it, the fresh new visual language has been created by making custom motifs inspired by sweets and savoury forms, and their transformation into unique patterns for each product range. It tows the very line of handcrafted goodness and technical excellence in every pattern created, just like in every delicacy they curate. "

Developed with Anomaly Brands.

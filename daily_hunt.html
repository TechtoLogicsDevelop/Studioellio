Learn more about the project

Dailyhunt's mission is to be the Indic platform empowering a billion Indians to discover, consume and socialise with the content that informs, enriches and entertains. As a News++ app, it provides a diverse range of content including entertainment, sports, local updates and more in over 14 languages across video and text format.


However, their identity failed to resonate with the youth outside the metro cities. The interface overwhelmed the target group that is not used to traditional news consumption. The challenge was to create a distinct identity that not only resonates with the offering but also stays true to the essence of Bharat.

To do so, the brand needed to fit into the life of its user by making the app more engaging, easier to use and fun. It needed to be as easily consumable as it is to scroll through Instagram, while maintaining the Indian-ness of it by celebrating the vernacular diversity.


Our interventions aim at making the brand stand out from its competitors. Inspired by vernacular letterforms and colours, we created a custom identity that showcased the youthful, dynamic side of the app complimented by a distinct palette. The identity taps into the nostalgic element of what once was, while also taking from what is now, that is, social media.

Packaging Design

Smoor- True Couverture Chocolates, is a luxury chocolate brand based in Bangalore that is well-known for its rich ingredients and trend-setting packaging. They offer a wide range of products from bars, to cakes and premium gift boxes with a luxurious, healthly and delectable experience.

The packaging design strategy was to create a carefully crafted artistic expression that would bring to life the powerful yet simple brand idea -- "True Chocolate. True Story."

The botanical design narrative for the packaging is an attempt to capture the splendour of nature and the product's rich ingredients, giving it a riveting, timeless look.it also sets a benchmark in chocolate packaging with its botanics language, which provides the brand with a range of unique designs across different categories of products.

Be sure to discover Smoor for the true chocolates, true stories and truly exquisite packaging.

Learn more about the project

PureCult, a natural home cleaning products range, is here to raise awareness, and stir the conversation around your cleaning practices. Cleaning isn't just about how clean the outcome is but also about how you clean. For PureCult clean spaces matters, the product used for cleaning matters, and above all, the environment matters! Therefore they have introduced a range of products with a difference, because you matter!


The brand strategy for PureCult was to build a brand that is honest, fuss-free, enpowering and conscious ,with a visual aim that is not only relevant but also contextual. It seeks to spark an interest in cleaning products with a fresh outlook and reinforce it beyond the conventional graphics and design. One of the core ideas is to encourage consumers to consider sustainable eco friendly cleaning alternatives.


This was brought to life with a vision of a safer, cleaner and greener earth. The idea was to acheive a mix of urban city life and clean green for more sustainable world. We have taken an illustrative approach to the brand's packaging, making it distinct from any other product in the category.


The client came to us with a custom mould for their products, and designs were created keeping in mind the bottle structure. Each section of the label has its own distinct function -- product name, variant, benefits of how a cleaner, greener earth could look.

However, due to production hurdles, the brand launched with adapts of these designs on generic bottles. They aim to launch their custom packs in phase two.

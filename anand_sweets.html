Learn more about the project

Anand Sweets & Savouries is a well-loved Indian mithai (sweets) and namkeen (savouries) business with a legacy of over 30 years in Bangalore.

The packaging design of the brand attempts to capture the essence of Indian folk crafts, which gives you diverse range of colours and techniques like bone inlay work, paper-cutting, and block print design among other things. With a range of about 300 products, the design language demands uniqueness and consistency to fit different tones of celebrations.


To encapsulate the everyday-Indian-luxury vibe, and add to the design's premium feel, Sanjhi, an Indian craft style, is used aesthetically with a touch of foiling.


With an innovative array of enticing creations, the aim of the packaging is to uplift the traditional status of mithai and namkeens, and merge it with a timeless design — all this while maintaining the inherent festivity of exchanging gifts with loved ones.


Anand Sweets makes luxury accessible to everyone with their packaging designs because customers are at the heart of what they do.
